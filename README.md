# BCnR Project

Website for the BCnR Project, an acoustic trio based in the Vacaville/Fairfield area of Northern California.

## About the Band

The BC&R Project features Brigette Winters, Charles Bookman, and Roy Owens playing acoustic covers of classic rock, blues, and alternative hits from the 60s to today. Formed in early 2025, the trio plays intimate gigs and wineries across the East Bay.

**Members:**
- Brigette — Lead Vocals
- Roy — Acoustic Guitar
- Charles — Bass

## Website Features

- **About** — Band bio and member listing
- **Music** — In-browser audio player with four tracks: *Overkill*, *Sara*, *I Do*, and *The Door*. Includes play/pause controls, a progress bar with click-to-seek and drag support, and an animated visualizer.
- **Upcoming Shows** — List of scheduled performances at venues including Downtown Joe's (Napa), Arc Guitar Festival (Winters), Dwain's Bar (Vacaville), Blue Lagoon Brewery (Vacaville), Turkovich Family Winery (Winters), Stonegate Country Club (Davis), and Hooby's Brewing (Winters).
- **Booking** — Contact email at booking@bcnrproject.com (assembled dynamically to reduce spam scraping).

## Tech Stack

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks or build tools required.
