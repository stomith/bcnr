BCnR Project

Listen

Hailing from Vacaville, Fairfield and 'Just Around the Corner,' the BC&R Project brings you Brigette Winters, Charles Bookman, and Roy Owens playing acoustic covers of timeless classic rock, blues, and alternative hits from the 60's until today. With over 100 years of experience between them, they're quite old – but still manage to get those fingers flying across the fretboard and those vocal cords belting! The BC&R Project formed in early 2025 with the desire to play more intimate gigs with the music they grew up with. They can be found playing smaller venues and wineries across the East Bay.

Brigette Lead Vocals
Roy Acoustic Guitar
Charles Bass
